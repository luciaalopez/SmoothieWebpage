Welcome to Smoothies

A smoothie is a drink made from pureed raw fruit and/or vegetables, using a blender. A smoothie often has a liquid base such as water, fruit juice, plant milk, and sometimes dairy products. Smoothies may be made using other ingredients like: crushed ice, sweeteners, whey powder, chocolate, nutritional supplements, and anything you like.
